Slow Cooker Chicken Tacos Recipe

Description

This slow cooker chicken tacos recipe is easy to make with just 3 ingredients for flavourful, super tender, shredded chicken. Spoon the chicken into warm tortillas for a very tasty meal any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons taco seasoning mix
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Combine chicken broth and taco seasoning mix in a bowl.

  3. Place chicken in a slow cooker. Pour chicken broth mixture over chicken.

  4. Cover slow cooker. Cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours. Shred chicken with two forks.

Cook's Note

Serve shredded chicken in warmed corn tortillas and top with shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes, sliced red onions, and cilantro.
